Fast, reliable, and accurate

Smarter Travel Assistant provide a help for customer with some difficulty with Singapore local language. Our translation system works as efficient as possible to ensure reliable translation service for our customer. Below is the example of the flowchart how our system works;

Visitors can bring in or email something in Chinese characters and Smarter Travel Assistant will translate it. Photos can be sent from visitors' phones to Smarter Travel Assistant's mobile number and the translation will be sent back by SMS within 5 minutes.
For example; if any of our client experience some difficulty with local language, they can always email the Chinese character they want to know at sta@gmail.com or SMS some picture they felt strange about at 6338 0880, then our staff will translate it and reply those client email/SMS within couple of minutes.

Marina Bay Sands

10 Bayfront Avenue
Singapore 018956
Tel: +65 6688 8888
Daily: 8:00 AM - 11:00 PM

Details

Marina Bay Sands is the most popular hotel in Singapore. It is because of the sky pool located at the hotel roof they called it Skypark. People always curious about the view from the top of this hotel, and make it as one of the visitor attraction place in Singapore. The view from the top of MBS hotel is truly amazing, there is no other greater feeling like standing at the top of the world, but only people who stays at the hotel can go to the top of the hotel and enjoying the views.

Gardens by the Bay

18 Marina Gardens Drive
Singapore 018953
Tel: +65 6420 6848
Day Pass: 9:00 AM – 8:00 PM daily, Annual Pass: 9:00 AM – 7:00 PB daily

Details

Gardens by the Bay is one of the nature park located near Marina Bay, Singapore. Many people always want to visit this place when going to Singapore. Gardens by the bay is unique designed park, so when people walking by the garden, it will attract people to go in. there are more than 50.000 types of flower inside the garden doom.

Shopping

Singapore is the heaven for shopping places. There are so many choices for shopping place located in Singapore. You will enjoy different experience of shopping for each different place for shopping in Singapore.

ION Orchard

ION Orchard, 2 Orchard Turn
Singapore 238801
Open daily 10am-10pm

Details

ION orchard brings together world-best brands in flagship, concept and lifestyles stores. There are more than 330 retail shops inside the mall. Fine brands like Gucci, L&V, Prada, Dior, Giorgio Armani, and Dolce&Gabbana are all available inside ION orchard.

Funan DigitaLife Mall

109 North Bridge Rd Singapore
Singapore 179097
10am – 10pm daily

Details

Funan is the favorite place to go for people who loves technology. This place consist of so many IT stores selling any IT items like accessories, laptop, handphone, headphones, and many more technology items. People said some items that are sold here is cheaper than the other places. Funan is a heaven for technology geeks. The mall serves free wireless internet.

Paragon

Paragon, 209 Orchard Rd
Singapore 238859

Details

Paragon mall is ideal for people who searching for fine desing of mall and luxury items. Paragon is an upmarket consist of more than 200 stores inside the mall. It has luxury item brands like Miumiu, Salvatore Feragamo, Gucci, Alfred Dunhill, Gucci, there are also some fashion items like Armani Exhange, Escada, G-Stars and many more. This place is surely for budget people to go to find something good.

Ngee An City

Ngee An City, 391 Orchard Rd.
Singapore 238872 

Details

Ngee An City is the place designed for everyone. People always go here to meet up with someone because it is the easiest place to find. You only need to go to orchard MRT then walk up and done. There is a fountain located on the center of the mall that make people easiest to meet up. The mall also has more than 100 specialty stores for example Cartier, Zara, Havaianas, Calvin Klein and other casual stores.
